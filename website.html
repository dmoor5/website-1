Computer CPUs

CPU or central prosessing unit are the brains of a computer and without them commputers would not be able to function properly. The CPUs job is to carry out the instructions of a computer program by performing the basic arithmetic, logical, control and input/output operations specified by the instructions.

Cpu Cores and Overclocking

Most CPUs have more than 1 core for multiple reasons. Cpu cores can be split up and dedicated to doing several tasks on a computerto increase the efficiancy of the computer. Overclocking is somthing someone might do to increase the preformance of their pc. It involves using more cores or threads than the prossesser is ment to use to get better megaherts or gigaherts per second which results in faster preformance for things that require higher end computers like gaming and video editing.
